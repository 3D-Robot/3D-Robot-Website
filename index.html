3D Robot

The robot is made of one or two arms on a omniwheel base that allows it to put any object of any material into any orientation. It has complete control of the xyz axis and the three rotations of rx ry rz. That is all of the six degrees of freedom!

Find out more

Connect

It is important to properly connect to parts to be able to move them around. We use our own system that allows us to get very good results (better then anyone else). This connector on the end of the robot allows us to accurately more around parts and toolheads to build complete systems!

Find out more

Collapsible 3D Printer / CNC

It is critical to be able to rapidly manufacture and create custom objects for this sytem to work. Every system is made up of objects in orientations. objects are made up of materials. There are a whole host of machines that are able to make these objects. Some of these processes (like 3d printing and light duty cnc routing and polishing) will be able to be done by the robot itself. These machines will be systems as part of the 3D Robot ecosystem and our goal is to have as many to be compatible with the 3D Robot to be able to create complete systems!

Find out more

Crypto

The goal of our ecosystem is to enable exponentially growing decentralized engineering and manufacturing.We believe the best and only way to do so is through the use of cutting edge technologies such as crypto and CAD softwares and open source collaborative design.In order to ensure that everyone is doing the right things we launched our 3DR-D token that will incentivize the design phase. There will also be End Products and Raw Materials that will help individuals get the systems and raw materials they need.
